Permanent Projects

Humanity wall

Rotary Club Unjha has made a clever move by asking people to place their unwanted goods at the collection center.The message is passed on through social media. This service will run forever, the needy can come to the center and take what they want without asking. Moreover, if generous amount of goods are collected, then the club will distribute them to the rural area
around the town.

 Some good pace, in , Unjha

Physiotherapy Center

Patients take benefit of physiotherapy center operated by Rotary Club Unjha. The Center is open all days of the week from 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM. Treatment given to all age groups at a lower cost. Center is developed inside organization building thus lowering the operating cost. In a month, about 500 people visit for treatment.

 Some good pace, in , Unjha

Drinking water stand

The human body contains 75% water, thus require a constant refill. Rotary Club Unjha is providing free mineral water every day since 2010 to everyone. The facility is constructed in the busiest area in town, near to railway station and bus station. Furthermore, on average 500 liters of water is consumed each day.

 Some good pace, in , Unjha

Public Bathroom and Toilet

One of the major problems faced by individuals while being out of home is sanitation. Rotary Club Unjha has developed pay and use public bathrooms and toilets. The facility is constructed opposite the water distribution centre mentioned above. Workers are appointed to be there all the time to keep the place clean. This project is helping many travellers and local people throughout the day.

 Some good pace, in , Unjha
